Images: 
Tag: <img> (No closing tag) 
        ex. <img src="mypicture.jpg" alt="Graphic of Web Words"> 

Alignment: 
        ex. <img src="picture.png" align="top, middle, bottom" alt="My Picture">
Spacing: 
        ex. <img src="picture.jpg" hspace="x" vspace="x" alt="My Picture">
Float: 
        ex. <img src="picture.gif" align="left, right" alt="My Picture">

Style: 
        ex. <img src="img_girl.jpg" alt="Girl in a jacket" style="width:500px;height:600px;">

Using Width and Height Attributes: 
        ex. <img src="img_girl.jpg" alt="Girl in a jacket" width="500" height="600">

New to HTML5: 
- <figure></figure> contains images and their caption 
- <figcaption></figcaption> is to add a caption to an image
